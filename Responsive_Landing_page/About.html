50 years of serving communities

Celebrating 50 years of serving communities, Starbucks continues to be more than just a coffee shop; it's a cultural phenomenon, a gathering place, and a global community builder. From its humble beginnings in Seattle to its now-iconic presence in nearly every corner of the world, Starbucks has played a significant role in shaping the way we enjoy coffee and connect with one another.

We’re looking forward to the next 50 years of Starbucks—working together with our partners, customers and communities to create a better future for people and the planet we all share.

We know our success – past, present, and future – is built on how we partner together. This culture of belonging is both our heritage and our future.

Starbucks dedication to diversity and inclusion has also been commendable. The company has taken significant strides in fostering an inclusive environment for both employees and customers, demonstrating that it values the uniqueness of each individual.
